Salesforce Tower by the numbers
Size: 61 stories, 1,070 feet, 1.42 million square feet
Height of floors: 14 feet, 9 inches
Tons of steel: 12,000
Curtain-wall panels: about 4,100
Workers on-site, at peak: 800
Parking spaces: 423 — 116 for cars, 307 for bicycles
Sidewalk security bollards: 137
Restrooms: 128
Elevators: 34, including a public one to the transit center’s rooftop park
Size of underground cistern to store rainwater: 50,000 gallons
